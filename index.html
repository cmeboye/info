About Me

Hey there Cem here! I’m currently pursuing a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering on Robotics track, basically trying to figure out do we make robots smarter, faster, and less likely to fall over.

I’ve dabbled in everything from designing robotic manipulators to optimizing wind turbine towers. My projects are usually a mix of CAD models, control algorithms, and a lot of late-night troubleshooting sessions. I’m a fan of hands-on learning, so whether it’s machining parts or coding in MATLAB, I dive right in (and usually swim back up after Googling a few things).

Outside of the lab, I’m into biking, running, and making abstract art when inspiration strikes. I like blending technical precision with creativity and self expression, whether that’s in engineering or in life.

If you’re curious about my work, projects, or just want to share some nerdy jokes, feel free to reach out. I’m always up for a chat (especially if it involves coffee).
